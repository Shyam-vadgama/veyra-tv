Veyra TV is a high-performance media engine for Android and TV.

Engineered for Speed

Built on top of Media3 (ExoPlayer). Experience instant start times and buttery smooth playback on any device, from flagship phones to Android TV boxes.

Private by Design

No analytics. No servers. Your playlist data lives on your device and nowhere else.

Native TV Support

Don't settle for mobile apps stretched on a TV screen. Veyra features a dedicated D-Pad navigation model designed for the 10-foot experience.

Open Source

Audit the code. Build it yourself. Contribute features. The power is in your hands.

Material You Design

A clean, dark-mode native UI that adheres to the latest Material Design 3 guidelines for a familiar and intuitive experience.
